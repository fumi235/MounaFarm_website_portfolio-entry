Arts & Culture

Arts help healing people.

Art

...helps heal, it is very important. Music, metal, ceramics, painting, sculpting, all traditions and forms.
The focus is on how we create. Sooriya brings artists to the village to share their art with the children and also work on their own art. Sharing knowledge and gaining support. Then they would return to their homes and start their own artist village, so more artist villages are created around the world.
